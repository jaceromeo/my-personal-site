Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

Originally from St. Louis, Missouri, I relocated to Chandler, Arizona in 2003. After 17 years of risk management in the financial services industry, I needed a change. I first became interested in coding after a conversation with a friend who is a software enigneer. His description of the opportunities that existed for those who are willing to learn a new skill eventually led me to HTML and CSS.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS clean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
